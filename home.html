How I Work

Principles that guide my approach.

01

Clarity Over Complexity

I specialize in taking the most complex, technical ideas and distilling them into narratives that feel human and accessible. If the story isn't simple, it's not ready.

02

Lead with Empathy

Whether navigating a tough launch or mentoring a teammate, I believe the best work happens when people feel heard and supported.

03

Speed with Precision

With a background in startups and big tech, I move fast — but always stay grounded in the user's perspective and the data that matters.

04

Build Bridges, Not Silos

My best work happens at the intersection — connecting engineering to marketing, partners to product, and communities to opportunity.
